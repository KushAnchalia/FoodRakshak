About Us

We provide solutions to minimize food waste at weddings by redistributing unused food and converting leftovers into organic manure.

Our Services

Food Collection

We collect unused and leftover food directly from wedding venues.

Redistribution

Unused food is distributed to nearby NGOs to feed the needy.

Manure Production

Leftover food is processed into organic manure for farmers.

Our Impact

Tons of food saved, families fed, and sustainable manure produced for farmers.
